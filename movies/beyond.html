Plot

A TOWN'S DARK PAST.

TWO DETECTIVES.

A RELENTLESS QUEST FOR JUSTICE.

"Beyond Evil" is a gripping psychological thriller that delves into the depths of human nature and the complexities of justice. The series follows two detectives, Lee Dong-sik and Han Joo-won, as they are drawn into the re-investigation of a cold case involving a series of gruesome murders that shook their small town years ago.

Overall

  • Story Arc and Continuity: 4/5
  • Character Development: 4.5/5
  • Writing and Dialogue: 4/5
  • Production Quality: 4/5
  • Emotional Impact and Entertainment Value: 4.5/5

My POV

For me, the brilliance of "Beyond Evil" lies in its intense character development and the way it unravels the hidden secrets of the town's residents. The relationship between Dong-sik and Joo-won is compelling, marked by tension, mistrust, and an evolving partnership. The plot is filled with unexpected twists and turns, keeping me on the edge of my seat as each episode reveals new layers of the mystery.

What drives someone to commit such heinous crimes?

And how far will the detectives go to uncover the truth?
