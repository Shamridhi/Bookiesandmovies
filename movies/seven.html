Plot

SEVEN DEADLY SINS.

TWO DETECTIVES.

A CHILLING HUNT FOR A SERIAL KILLER.

"Se7en" is a dark and gripping psychological thriller that follows detectives David Mills and William Somerset as they pursue a brutal and methodical serial killer who uses the seven deadly sins as his modus operandi. Directed by David Fincher, the film delves into the sinister depths of human nature and morality.

My POV

For me, "Se7en" stands out due to its intense atmosphere, masterful storytelling, and unforgettable performances by Brad Pitt and Morgan Freeman. The film's exploration of sin, justice, and the human condition is both disturbing and thought-provoking. The suspense builds relentlessly as the detectives uncover the gruesome crime scenes, each representing a different sin, leading to a shocking and haunting climax. The dark, rain-soaked cityscape and the film's meticulous attention to detail create a sense of dread that lingers long after the credits roll.

How far will a killer go to make a statement?

And can the detectives stop him before he completes his twisted plan?
