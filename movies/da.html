Plot

A KIDNAPPING WITHOUT A MOTIVE.

A 15-YEAR IMPRISONMENT.

A QUEST FOR REVENGE.

"Oldboy" is a gripping thriller that follows Oh Dae-su, a man who is inexplicably kidnapped and imprisoned for 15 years without knowing his captor or the reason behind his confinement. Upon his sudden release, Dae-su embarks on a relentless journey to uncover the identity of his tormentor and the motive behind his abduction. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he discovers shocking truths and faces harrowing challenges, leading to a climactic and unforgettable conclusion.

My POV

For me, the allure of "Oldboy" lies in its intense and unpredictable storyline, combined with its profound exploration of themes such as revenge, redemption, and the consequences of one's actions. The film's director, Park Chan-wook, masterfully blends brutal action with deep emotional complexity, creating a gripping and unforgettable cinematic experience. The lead actor, Choi Min-sik, delivers a powerful performance as Oh Dae-su, a man driven by an insatiable need to uncover the truth behind his inexplicable 15-year imprisonment. The film's shocking twists and thought-provoking narrative keep viewers captivated until the very end.
