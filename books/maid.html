Plot

At the heart of the story is Mariana, a brilliant but troubled group therapist who becomes fixated on the suspicious activities surrounding a secret society called The Maidens.

Her obsession intensifies when one of its members is found murdered. Determined to uncover the truth, she dives deep into the lives of the seemingly perfect students and their charismatic professor, uncovering secrets that someone is willing to kill to protect

How far would you go to uncover the truth?

Can Mariana solve the mystery before it's too late?

For me, the heart of the story is Mariana, a brilliant but troubled therapist who becomes obsessed with the secretive activities of The Maidens.

When one of their members is murdered, her fixation intensifies, leading her to investigate the seemingly perfect students and their charismatic professor. As she digs deeper, she uncovers dark secrets worth killing for.

I found Mariana's intense and psychologically complex journey absolutely captivating, making this book a must-read for any thriller fan.
