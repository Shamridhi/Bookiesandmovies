Plot

WALK INTO DETENTION.

ONLY FOUR LEAVE ALIVE.

This is a great suspense thriller and a classic 'whodunnit'. It reveals the dark sides of people who are believed to have perfect lives and exposes their secrets. This book has been adapted into a Netflix series.

The best lines to describe the book for me would be:

Everyone has secrets, but who has gone this far to protect them?.

How far will you go to protect your secret?
