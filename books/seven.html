"The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo" is a captivating novel that tells the story of Evelyn Hugo, a reclusive Hollywood icon, as she reveals the truth about her glamorous and scandalous life. The narrative is framed through her interview with Monique Grant, a relatively unknown journalist chosen by Evelyn to write her biography.

My POV

For me, the allure of this book lies in Evelyn's unapologetic recounting of her life, filled with ambition, heartbreak, and love. Each husband represents a different chapter and facet of her journey, but it's the hidden love story that truly resonated with me. The novel's exploration of identity, sacrifice, and the complexities of love kept me enthralled from start to finish.

Who was Evelyn Hugo's true love?

And why did she choose Monique to tell her story?
