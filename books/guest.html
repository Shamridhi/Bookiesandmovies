Plot

Off the windswept Irish coast, guests gather for the wedding of the year.

The setting is spectacular, the atmosphere alive. But as the guest toast the golden couple, dark secrets begin to spill. And the wedding cake has barely been cut when someone is found dead.

As the storm unleashes its fury on the island, everyone is trapped - and the killer circulates amongst the guests.

My POV

I would like to give a tagline to this book like

  • IT STARTS WITH A PARTY.
  • IT'LL END IN MURDER.

This is a 'whodunnit' kind of read where all the characters are suspects. For me, this is the most interesting part, as each of them has a strong motive and plenty to hide.

The key is to guess who actually did it and why. The best thing about these books is that they make us feel like detectives, piecing together clues.

  • No one can enter.
  • No one can leave.
